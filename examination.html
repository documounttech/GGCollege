Examination Guidelines

For Students

  • Fill examination forms before the due date
  • Carry admit card and ID card to exam hall
  • Arrive 15 minutes before exam starts
  • No electronic devices allowed in exam hall
  • Follow examination rules and regulations
  • Report any discrepancies immediately
  • Maintain discipline during examinations

Exam Hall Rules

  • Maintain silence in the examination hall
  • Mobile phones strictly prohibited
  • No communication with other students
  • Write roll number on every answer sheet
  • Do not leave hall without permission
  • Submit answer sheets before leaving
  • Malpractice will lead to cancellation
