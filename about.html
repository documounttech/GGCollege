About Our College

Excellence in Education Since Establishment

Welcome to Giriraj Government College

Giriraj Government College (Autonomous), located in Nizamabad, Telangana, stands as a beacon of quality education and academic excellence. Affiliated to Telangana University and reaccredited with 'B' Grade by NAAC, our institution is committed to providing holistic education that prepares students for the challenges of the modern world.

Our Heritage

Foundation and Growth

Established with the vision of providing quality higher education to the youth of Nizamabad and surrounding regions, Giriraj Government College has grown into a premier institution of learning. Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while adapting to the evolving needs of students and society.

Our autonomous status enables us to design innovative curricula, implement modern teaching methodologies, and provide a learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and research aptitude among students.

Recognition and Accreditation

Our institution takes pride in being reaccredited with 'B' Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), which is a testament to our unwavering commitment to maintaining high academic standards and providing quality education.

As an institution affiliated to Telangana University, we ensure that our academic programs meet the highest standards of excellence and are aligned with national educational objectives.
