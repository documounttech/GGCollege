About RTI Act 2005

The Right to Information Act, 2005 empowers citizens to access information from public authorities and promotes transparency and accountability in the working of government institutions.

As a government institution, Giriraj Government College is committed to providing information to citizens as per the RTI Act provisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can file an RTI application?

Any Indian citizen can file an RTI application to seek information from the college.

Q: Is there a specific format?

No specific format required. Application should be in writing with clear information sought.

Q: What if I don't get a response?

You can file a first appeal to the Appellate Authority within 30 days.

Q: Can I request information orally?

No, RTI applications must be submitted in writing.
