National Institutional Ranking Framework

About NIRF

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) was approved by the MHRD and launched by Hon'ble Minister of Human Resource Development on September 29, 2015. This framework outlines a methodology to rank institutions across the country.

Giriraj Government College participates in NIRF rankings to maintain transparency and demonstrate our commitment to quality education.
